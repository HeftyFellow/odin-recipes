General Tso's Chicken

Description

Today we will teach you how to make General Tso's Chicken.

Ingredients

  • Chili
  • Cornflour/Cornstarch
  • Hoisin
  • Sesame Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Chicken Stock/Broth
  • Rice Vinegar

Steps

  1. Sauce/Marinade
  2. Marinate Chicken
  3. Coat Chicken
  4. Shake Off Excess
  5. Cook Chicken Using Preferred Amount of Oil
  6. Drain On Paper Towel
  7. Saute Garlic, Ginger and chili flakes
  8. Simmer Sauce
  9. Add chicken
  10. Quickly toss to coat then serve
