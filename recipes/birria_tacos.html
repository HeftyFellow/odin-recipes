Birria Tacos

Description

Today we will be showing you how to make the best Birria Tacos.

Ingredients

  • Short Ribs (BONE IN!)
  • Carrots
  • Bay Leaves
  • Oregano
  • Cumin
  • Salt
  • Chili Powder
  • White Onion
  • Garlic
  • Corn Tortillas
  • Chicken Bouillon
  • Quesadilla Cheese
  • Guajillo Peppers
  • Chuck Roast

Steps

  1. Simmer
  2. Blend Peppers
  3. Continue to simmer
  4. Chop the meat
  5. Use leftover grease
  6. Fry tortillas
  7. Serve with consomme
